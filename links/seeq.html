SEEQ

Energy Club, Center for Energy and Environment, MNIT Jaipur is delighted to announce its annual Sustainable Energy and Environment Quiz 2016, to be held on August 20-21, 2016 at MNIT Jaipur.The event once again invites environment geeks and green enthusiasts to display their talent and win exciting prizes. So come and be a part of this Green Extravaganza.
8th Sustainable Energy & Environment Quiz has been successfully organised by the Energy Club Team, MNIT Jaipur. More than 200 teams participated in the first round, and 30 teams got selected for the 2nd round. Finally, top 6 teams competed in the third round to win the Event.
Top 3 teams are as follows:-

  1. Harsh Vardhan Singh Rathore, Ram Govind Yadav
  2. Vedant Kumar Pankaj, Vipul Agarwal
  3. Bharat Kumar Meena
Hearty congratulations to all the winners. We appreciate the participation of all teams who made the quiz so competitive and successful. We wish you for your bright future.Hope to see you all in our next event Greenovation.
